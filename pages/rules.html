General Rules

Symphony caters to over 20,000 attendees each year. Each attendee has availed the benefit of watching various cultural events and entertainment while maintaining caution during the 3-day fest aiding us in enabling the safety of the crowd. With this being said in the overview, follow the guideline laid below.

General Rules

  • Participation is open for all the events in Virtual Mode.
  • For Offline Events/Finales attendance, participants need to have a double vaccinated certificate or within 72-hour RT-PCR report.
  • Entry in the campus will not be permitted without any of the documents in the above mentioned point and will be solely participants responsibility .
  • All rules must be strictly adhered for each and every event during the competition.
  • Any kind of religious/political/controversial/offensive/vulgar depiction is strictly prohibited.
  • Judge's collective decision will be final and binding. No doubt regarding the same shall be entertained.
  • Judge scores or mark sheets will not be revealed under any circumstances, for both virtual and offline events.
  • Submissions won't be accepted once the deadline lapses.
  • Exceeding the time limit will result in negative marking and/or disqualification.
  • The organizers will contact the participants only via the email address and contact numbers given during registration.
  • The information provided during registration should be correct, and is the sole responsibility of the participant.
  • Requests for registration fee refunds shall not be entertained. If under any circumstances, the competition is canceled by the organizers then the registration fees will be credited back.
  • Symphony does not intend any copyright claims on the artist's work. The winner's art, if posted on the social media handles of Symphony, shall have the due credits to the creators.
  • The organizing committee reserves the right to change the prize structure, prize money, nature of prize at any point of time before the start of the event/competition.
  • In case of cancellation of any event, the registration fees will be refunded within 15 days of cancellation.
  • The same team participating in the eliminations should take part in the final rounds. Any substitutions would lead to disqualification.
  • Display of violence and unruly behaviour shall lead to disqualification and expulsion of the individual and the respective college from the festival.
  • Decisions taken by the judges are final and irrevocable for all of the events.
  • Violation of any of the rules mentioned in the rulebook shall lead to the disqualification of the participant(s).
  • The organizers reserve the right to reduce the duration of the performance in the elimination rounds in case there is a shortage of time due to the number of performing teams.
  • The decisions of the organizing committee will be final and binding under all circumstances.
  • All updates will be posted on our social media handles and our official website regularly.
